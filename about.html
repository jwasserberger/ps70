# bio
    
# --------------------------------------------------------------------------------

i grew up in sydney, australia. for my undergrad, i studied civil engineering becoming involved in sustainability and water infrastructure. after graduating, i worked as a dams and hydropower engineer in sydney and london. five years and a pandemic later, i needed a change. i wanted to find more creative and rapid ways of building technology to solve problems for others. that led me to the design engineering program at harvard. ps70 is a chance for me to learn the skills i wish i had been taught in undergrad!

at home, i spend my time doing something related to food, wine, music, basketball, design and architecture. at school, i'm doing something related to sustainability, climate adaptation, waste, regenerative agriculture, and soil health.
